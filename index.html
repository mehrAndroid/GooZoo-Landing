You’ve been invited to a team — not just an app.

GooZoo helps you organize a real operational team of friends and supporters. You practice how to move together, stay active, and build trust — step by step. When your team is ready, you can enter the next stage together: Follow Me, where groups can define internet-based projects and share results like a true championship squad.

What GooZoo is for

In today’s world, income and opportunity are no longer limited to the physical world. Massive resources flow in the digital space — and to capture them, we must learn to work as a team. GooZoo is the training ground: build your team here, organize it, and prepare for Follow Me.

  • Create your team from people you actually know.
  • Stay active through simple invitations and friendly messages.
  • Build trust through consistent, human interaction — not empty numbers.
  • Practice teamwork like training for a competition.
  • Move together to Follow Me when your network is ready.

WHAT DOES “GOOZOO” MEAN? GOOZOO is made of two parts: GOO — GOAL-ORIENTED ORGANIZATION Move with purpose, not confusion. Know why we are together, and where we’re going. ZOO — ZENITH OUTREACH OUTCOMES Reach real outcomes through teamwork, support, and moving together. Not blind competition. Not climbing on other people’s shoulders. AND YES… ONE FUNNY DETAIL 🙂 In Persian, “GooZoo” can sound like a playful word that makes people smile. But here, it’s up to you what you want it to mean. For some people, GooZoo is simply a warm way to send a friendly, human message. For others, it’s the start of a bigger journey.

HOW DOES GOOZOO WORK? In GooZoo, a network grows slowly — but it grows for real: • People follow each other because they actually know each other • Interaction matters more than numbers • Trust is built step by step WHERE DOES GOOZOO START? With a simple human message. You can: • write a friendly invitation text • choose a photo or a short video • add gentle music if you want • preview exactly what the other person will receive Then you decide how to send it: WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, email, or any other app. WHEN THE NETWORK IS READY When a network: • reaches a solid level of trust • stays active • and becomes able to move as a group the members can request to enter the next stage together: FOLLOW ME WHAT IS FOLLOW ME? Follow Me is a platform for collective movement. At this stage: • shared goals are defined as internet-based projects or businesses • the network works toward those goals using Follow Me tools • the results are shared among the contributors Here: • profit comes from real activity • not promises • not just recruiting people
